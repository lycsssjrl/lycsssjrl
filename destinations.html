Explore the Famous Volcanoes in the Philippines

Mount Mayon

Famous for its near-perfect cone shape, Mayon is an active volcano in the Bicol region. It is a popular destination for hikers and adventure seekers.

Activities: Hiking, Lava Viewing, ATV Rides, and enjoying the view from Legazpi City.

Taal Volcano

Located in the middle of Taal Lake, Taal Volcano is one of the most active in the Philippines. It has a stunning caldera with a small lake inside.

Activities: Boating to the island, hiking, and exploring the crater lake.

Mount Pinatubo

Pinatubo erupted dramatically in 1991, and today visitors can trek to its crater lake, formed by the eruption, and enjoy its surreal beauty.

Activities: Trekking to the crater lake, off-road driving, and enjoying the panoramic views.

Mount Apo

The highest mountain in the Philippines, Mount Apo is a popular trekking destination for experienced hikers. It is an active volcano with diverse ecosystems.

Activities: Hiking, camping, and exploring the diverse flora and fauna.

Mount Kanlaon

Located in Negros Island, Kanlaon is an active stratovolcano. It is the highest peak in the Visayas and a popular climbing destination.

Activities: Hiking, camping, and exploring the nearby waterfalls and hot springs.

Mount Bulusan

Located in Sorsogon, Mount Bulusan is an active stratovolcano that offers an exciting hike through forests and volcanic terrain. It has an accessible crater lake.

Activities: Hiking, exploring the volcanic crater lake, and visiting nearby hot springs.
