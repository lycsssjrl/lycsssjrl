Explore the Famous Beaches in the Philippines

El Nido, Palawan

El Nido, located in the northern part of Palawan, Philippines, is one of the country's most stunning destinations, renowned for its dramatic limestone cliffs, crystal-clear waters, pristine beaches, and vibrant marine life.

Activities:

Island Hopping
Snorkeling
Diving
Kayaking
Beach Hopping
Hiking

Coron Island, Palawan

Coron Island is a beautiful and remote island in the Calamian Archipelago, located in Palawan, Philippines. It is part of Coron Town in Busuanga, Palawan, and is known for its stunning natural beauty, crystal-clear lakes, dramatic limestone cliffs, and rich marine biodiversity. Coron is also famous for its wreck diving sites, making it a top destination for diving enthusiasts..

Activities:

Group Tour
Coron Reefs
Wreck Tour
Snorkeling
Island Hopping
